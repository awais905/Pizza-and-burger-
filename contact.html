Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you serve?

We serve in Shahjmal, Mondka, Shahgarh, Khangarh, and Muzaffargarh. Delivery charges may vary based on distance.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM every day. Orders after 10:00 PM will be delivered the next day.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events and parties. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for large orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and mobile payments. Online payment options are coming soon.
