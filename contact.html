Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our restaurant

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all areas within Shahjmal and surrounding neighborhoods. Delivery charges may vary based on distance. Contact us directly to confirm if we deliver to your location.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and mobile payments. For online orders, we also accept credit/debit cards through secure payment gateways.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your catering needs and menu options.

Can I customize my pizza or burger?

Absolutely! We welcome customizations to suit your taste preferences. You can add or remove ingredients, choose your spice level, and request special preparations when placing your order.
