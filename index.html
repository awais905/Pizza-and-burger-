About Us

Our Story

At Pizza & Burger, we've been serving the best pizza and burgers in South Punjab since 2010. Our passion for quality ingredients and authentic recipes has made us a favorite among locals in Shahjmal, Mondka, Shahgarh, Khangarh, and Muzaffargarh.

We believe that great food brings people together, and we're committed to creating memorable dining experiences for our customers. Every pizza and burger is prepared with care and the freshest ingredients available.
