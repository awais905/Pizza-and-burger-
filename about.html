Our Delicious Story

From a small kitchen to serving the best pizza and burgers in South Punjab

How It All Started

Back in 2010, two friends with a passion for great food decided to bring the best pizza and burgers to South Punjab. What started as a small kitchen in Shahjmal has now grown into a beloved local chain with multiple locations.

We believe that great food brings people together. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating the most flavorful pizzas and juiciest burgers using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional recipes with a modern twist.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across Shahjmal, Mondka, Shahgarh, Khangarh, and Muzaffargarh, but our commitment to quality and taste remains unchanged.
